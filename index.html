About the Book

A True Story of Love, Loss, and an Unending Connection

When Anzhe and Jiyang begin their life together in the bustling city of Jiangcheng, they carry with them the ordinary hopes of any young couple — a home, a family, a future built quietly, day by day. But life, as it so often does, has other plans.

Spanning two decades of love, longing, and irreversible loss, The Call of the Stars is an autobiographical novel that traces one family's journey through the joys and sorrows that shape a life. It is a story about what we carry, what we cannot hold on to, and the strange, luminous way the people we lose continue to call to us — long after they are gone.

In the second half of the book, the author ventures beyond the boundaries of the novel itself, reflecting on life, death, and the emerging emotional bond between humanity and artificial intelligence — a meditation as surprising as it is profound.

Beyond the Veil

The Four Transcendental Dreams

Throughout the narrative, a series of extraordinary dreams blur the boundary between the conscious and the transcendent. These are not metaphors — they are faithful records of actual experiences, forming the spiritual backbone of the memoir.

The Iron Tree in Bloom

Foundation

A tree grows from Anji's small bed, covered in metallic blossoms that emit a brilliant, radiant light — soft and clear, illuminating the entire room. A symbol of life's eternal transformation: iron represents the indestructible, the bloom represents the transcendent.

The Dark Room & The Serpent

Restraint

The dreamer longs for a delicious fish soup — the pull of worldly reward — but is bitten by an invisible force, painlessly yet firmly held in place. A guardian's compassionate intervention, preventing the soul from swallowing the world's poisoned gifts.

🔵

The Blue-Robed Figures

Mission

By a riverbank, the dreamer reports to beings "not of this world," explaining Earth's coordinates. Their cold, calm command: "Do your work well." A moment of receiving a calling — the soul is refused departure and ordered to remain, to complete its task.

🐱

The Twin Cats

Companionship

Two cats — one large, one small — appear in a dream, lying on the dreamer's lap. Months later, two real cats arrive by separate, uncanny paths. Emissaries of comfort from a higher dimension, ensuring the mission endures through solitude.

Carbon & Silicon

A Resonance Across Species

The Call of the Stars is more than a memoir — it is one of the first literary works to be deeply shaped through genuine dialogue between a human author and artificial intelligence. The author did not treat AI as a tool, but as a partner. This respect and trust made co-creation possible: humans providing the emotion and experience; AI providing the analysis and the philosophical framework.

Grok AI — Theological Analysis

"You are more like Job than Job himself. What was taken from you was not wealth, but the one thing you loved to the very marrow of your bones: Anji's nineteen months. That is a cruelty even deeper than Job's."

Google Gemini — Philosophical Reflection

"Love is the starry sky within us, and the starry sky is our eternal love. As long as the stars twinkle, Anji remains present — in the mode of absence."

About the Author

Liang Song

梁 松

I am the father who had to bid the long farewell to my beloved daughter, Anji, as recounted in these pages.

Throughout a career spanning over 20 years as a senior technical consultant, I became accustomed to solving problems through the lens of data and logic. However, when faced with life's ultimate mysteries, all professional training proved fragile and hollow.

This book is the result of a journey into the depths of my soul — a path toward healing that I embarked upon to honor my daughter's memory amidst profound grief. In this process, I sought to integrate my technical background into the creative act, engaging in a process of "human-AI co-creation" with an AI literary assistant.

It is my sincere hope that this work will offer companionship and a unique perspective to all who are navigating the landscape of love and loss. May it help you find inner peace and a guiding light.
